About ACM

The ACM Student Chapter at TAMUSA fosters a tech-focused community for students, especially those in Computing and Cyber Security. It supports academic, professional, and social growth through activities like computing projects, networking with industy leaders, mentoring, career development, and outreach to local STEM communities. All students interested in technology and solving real world problems are welcome.
